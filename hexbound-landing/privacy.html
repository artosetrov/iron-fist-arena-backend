Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Hexbound: PvP Arena RPG ("Hexbound", "we", "our", or "us") is developed by Artem Osetrov. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information from those services.
  • Game Data: We store your game progress, including characters, items, combat history, achievements, and in-game currency balances on our servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for debugging and compatibility purposes.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you enable push notifications, we collect your device token to send you game updates and notifications.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game experience
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To sync your game progress across sessions
  • To send push notifications about game events (if enabled)
  • To improve game balance, fix bugs, and enhance features
  • To prevent cheating and ensure fair play

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored securely on servers hosted by Supabase (powered by AWS). We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data transmission. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Hexbound uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication infrastructure
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method
  • Sign in with Apple: Optional authentication method
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): For delivering push notifications

Children's Privacy

Hexbound is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time through your device settings
  • Request a copy of your data

Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
