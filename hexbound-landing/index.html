Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before entering the realm

Is Hexbound pay-to-win?
Absolutely not. Hexbound follows a "monetization = acceleration" model. Every item and advantage can be earned through gameplay. Premium currency speeds up progression but never gives exclusive power. Ranked arena matchmaking is based on ELO rating, not gear score — strategy always beats spending.
What platforms is Hexbound available on?
Hexbound is currently available on iOS via the App Store. The game is optimized for iPhone and iPad. Android and other platform releases are planned for future updates.
How does PvP matchmaking work?
PvP uses an ELO-based rating system. You start at 800 rating and climb through brackets: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Grandmaster (3000+). Matchmaking pairs you with opponents of similar rating, ensuring fair and competitive battles.
Can I play without internet?
Hexbound is a server-authoritative game — all combat, rewards, and progression are verified server-side to prevent cheating. An internet connection is required for all game modes including PvP arena and dungeon runs.
What is the Prestige system?
When you reach level 50, you can Prestige — resetting your level to 1 while gaining a permanent +5% stat bonus. Each Prestige unlocks a new title tier: Reborn → Awakened → Ascended → Transcended → Eternal. There is no prestige cap — infinite mastery awaits.
How long is a typical play session?
Hexbound is designed for 2–5 minute sessions. A single PvP match takes about 2 minutes. Dungeon runs are longer (5–15 minutes depending on difficulty). You can check your Gold Mine and manage gear in under a minute.
