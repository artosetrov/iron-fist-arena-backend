A single isolated hexagonal magical seal for use as a loading icon in a dark fantasy mobile RPG. The icon must be easily cut out from the background — clean edges, thick outlines, no stray elements bleeding off the frame.

CENTER: A hexagonal frame made of dark forged iron with visible rivets, weld marks, and fine crosshatch shading giving the metal weight and texture. The hexagon edges are slightly uneven — hand-forged, not machine-perfect. Each of the six corners has a small iron spike or thorn protruding outward. Faint cracks run through the metal, and a greenish-bronze patina sits in the recesses.

INSIDE THE HEXAGON: A glowing arcane seal — concentric geometric patterns drawn in golden luminous ink (#D4A537 core, #FFD700 brightest highlights). The innermost shape is a smaller hexagon rotated 30 degrees, creating a star-like overlap. Between the inner and outer hexagons — six runic glyphs evenly spaced, each glowing gold, drawn in an ancient hand-scratched style as if carved by a knife into stone. The runes pulse with warm light. At the very center — a single bright point of golden-white light radiating thin light rays outward, like a magical core powering the seal.

AROUND THE HEXAGON: Six small orbiting arcane fragments — tiny broken rune stones or crystal shards floating just outside the hexagon frame, each trailing a faint golden particle trail as if slowly rotating around the seal. These fragments glow with the same golden energy (#D4A537). They are small — accent details, not dominant.

SURFACE DETAILS: The iron frame has micro-engravings — tiny hexagonal patterns repeated along the flat edges of the frame, visible only at close inspection. Small chain links dangle from the bottom two corners of the hexagon — broken chains, as if the seal was once bound and has been unleashed.

LIGHTING: The golden glow from the inner seal illuminates the iron frame from within, creating warm rim lighting on the metal edges. The glow casts no shadow — the icon is a self-contained object.

BACKGROUND: Pure solid black (#000000). No gradients, no atmospheric effects, no ground, no shadows on the background — completely clean for easy cutout.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark iron gray frame, greenish-bronze patina, golden glow (#D4A537, #FFD700) as the dominant luminous accent, faint warm orange in the deepest rune carvings, bright golden-white at the center point. Minimal colors — the gold-on-dark-iron contrast is the identity.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, pen-and-ink linework with watercolor wash coloring, crosshatching shading on the metal frame, thick bold outlines with varying line weight, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style.
