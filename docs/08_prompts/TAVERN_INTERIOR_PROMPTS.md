# Hexbound — Промпты для Nano Banana Pro: Tavern Interior + Minigame Assets

> **Status**: `canonical` — Active prompt collection. Indexed at `docs/08_prompts/ASSET_PROMPTS_INDEX.md`

## Назначение

Фоновая иллюстрация для экрана **Tavern** (меню мини-игр) + иконки мини-игр.
Формат фона: **9:16** (вертикальный, полноэкранный на iPhone).
Формат иконок: **1:1** (512×512).

Таверна — игровой зал внутри тёмного фэнтези мира. Здесь путешественники пьют, играют в азартные игры и бросают вызов судьбе. Это НЕ уютный трактир — это мрачный притон с азартными играми, освещённый факелами и свечами.

## Стиль-референс

Стиль **идентичен hub city map**: pen and ink illustration с акварельной заливкой поверх туши. Жирные чёрные контуры видны на каждом элементе. Заливка — мазками, не плоская, видна текстура бумаги/акварели. Тени через затемнение заливки и crosshatching, НЕ через blur/glow. Палитра: приглушённые землистые тона (коричневый, серый камень, тёмная охра) + 1-2 тёплых акцента (золото-оранжевый от огня/фонарей). Портреты — на фоне состаренного пергамента, гротескные черты лица, преувеличенные пропорции, как иллюстрации из D&D Monster Manual.

## 1. `bg-tavern` — Tavern Interior Background (9:16)

```
Pen and ink illustration with watercolor wash coloring of a grimdark medieval fantasy tavern interior, vertical composition 9:16 for mobile game background. Viewed from slightly elevated angle looking down into the room. Heavy bold black ink outlines on every architectural element. Watercolor washes in muted earth tones fill the ink linework.

ARCHITECTURE: Stone walls with gothic pointed arches and heavy stone pillars — gray ink crosshatching for stone texture, dark mortar lines between blocks. Vaulted ceiling with dark wooden beams. The tavern is built inside an old stone hall — NOT a wooden cabin. Stone floor with rough wooden plank patches and gaps.

BACK WALL (bar area): A long heavy wooden bar counter with an imposing bartender behind it — a large brutish orc or ogre silhouette with broad shoulders, wiping a tankard. Shelves behind the bar filled with dusty bottles, green and blue glass catching dim light — teal-green watercolor wash for bottle glass. Kegs stacked on the sides. A mounted beast skull with horns above the shelves.

RIGHT SIDE: A large stone fireplace with a real fire burning — warm orange-red watercolor glow illuminating nearby stone. A skeleton sitting on a stool by the fireplace playing a lute — bones rendered with bold ink outlines, tattered clothes. Barrels near the fireplace, one open showing a pile of gold coins inside — warm gold watercolor wash. Candles on wall brackets.

LEFT SIDE: A tall narrow stained glass window with colored geometric panels — muted jewel-tone watercolor (deep red, blue, green, gold) glowing faintly with outside light. A stone archway leading to a dark corridor or cellar entrance. A dogfolk patron (anthropomorphic dog person in armor) leaning against the wall near the arch, arms crossed — ink silhouette with minimal detail.

FOREGROUND (bottom third): A table edge visible at the very bottom — someone playing a shell game with three cups, a hand placing a gold coin as a bet. Scattered gold coins on the floor. Beer mugs. Wooden stools.

CENTER ZONE (critical): The middle 50-60% of image should be relatively DARK and OPEN — this is where translucent game UI cards will overlay. A central wooden table with a lantern and scattered items is acceptable but keep it dim. Characters sitting around tables are fine but rendered as ink silhouettes — a hooded demon figure, cloaked humans, a dwarf with an axe. Do NOT place bright or detailed focal objects in the dead center.

ATMOSPHERE: Dim and moody. Warm golden-orange light from fireplace and scattered lanterns creates pools of light with deep shadows between them. Visible smoke wisps drawn as thin ink lines curling from pipes and fireplace. The feeling is: a dangerous gathering place where all races mix — orcs, skeletons, demons, humans, dogfolk. NOT cozy — menacing, secretive, full of gambling and scheming.

COLOR PALETTE: Very dark base (near-black stone gray #1A1A2E and dark brown #2A1F14). Warm golden-orange (#D4A537) for firelight and lanterns. Gray stone with blue-gray shadows. Dark brown watercolor for wood. Teal-green for bottle glass. Muted jewel tones in stained glass only. Rust-red for blood stains and wine.

STYLE: Pen and ink illustration with watercolor wash, NOT pixel art, NOT digital painting, NOT concept art. Bold black ink outlines on every element visible at all scales. Watercolor fills with visible brush texture. Crosshatching for shadows, stone texture, wood grain. Grimdark dark fantasy tavern — grotesque characters, weathered surfaces, nothing clean or new. No blur, no glow effects, no fog, no text, no UI elements, no readable signs.
```

## Примечания по использованию

- **`bg-tavern`** используется как `Image("bg-tavern")` в `TavernDetailView.swift` — растягивается на весь экран под UI карточками мини-игр (аналогично `bg-hub` в `CityMapView`)
- **Иконки мини-игр** заменят текущие SF Symbol иконки (`checkmark.circle`, `hurricane`, `person.slash`) в карточках `TavernGameCard`
- **Hero patrons** — опциональные декоративные элементы, не блокируют основную функциональность
- **Стиль**: pen and ink + watercolor wash — как hub city map и портрет ведьмы из референсов. НЕ digital painting, НЕ concept art
- **Портреты**: на состаренном пергаменте, гротескные преувеличенные черты, как Monster Manual
- **Иконки**: на чёрном фоне (для тёмного UI)
- Все ассеты генерировать через Nano Banana Pro (или DALL-E) с указанием стиля из `ART_STYLE_GUIDE.md`
