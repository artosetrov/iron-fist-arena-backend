# Hexbound — промпты для Nano Banana Pro

> **Status**: `canonical` — Active prompt collection. Indexed at `docs/08_prompts/ASSET_PROMPTS_INDEX.md`

Стиль: формат и композиция как у логотипов WoW/Hearthstone (рамка-щит, объёмные буквы, декоративные элементы), НО выполненный в стиле игры — hand-drawn ink illustration, crosshatching, гротескные персонажи, мрачный комиксовый aesthetic. Не полированный 3D, а стилизованная тушь + акварель.

---

## 1. Основной логотип (splash screen / логин-экран)

```
Create a fantasy video game logo for "HEXBOUND" that has the composition and structure of World of Warcraft or Hearthstone logos (decorative shield frame, large title text, subtitle banner) BUT drawn entirely in a hand-drawn ink illustration and crosshatching art style — NOT polished 3D digital painting.

The word "HEXBOUND" in large stylized fantasy lettering that looks hand-drawn with ink — the letters have a golden-bronze color with crosshatch shading to create depth and volume, thick dark ink outlines, and slightly rough organic edges as if drawn by hand on parchment. The letters have subtle arcane rune engravings scratched into their surface.

Behind the text, a decorative shield-shaped frame with a hexagonal silhouette — drawn in the same ink crosshatch style. The frame looks like hand-drawn ornate metalwork: dark iron with bronze filigree, rivets, thorny vine details curling around the edges — all rendered with detailed crosshatching and ink line work, not smooth digital gradients. Subtle cracks and weathering drawn with fine ink lines.

At the top center of the frame: a hexagonal gemstone drawn in ink style — dark purple with golden glow lines radiating outward, set in an ornate hand-drawn metal mount.

Small thematic details in the same ink illustration style: chain links hanging from the frame sides, tiny skull ornaments at the corners, bony skeleton fingers gripping the bottom of the frame from below.

Below the main title on a hand-drawn banner plate: "FORGE YOUR FATE" in smaller matching lettering.

The ENTIRE logo — text, frame, details — must be in consistent hand-drawn ink and crosshatch illustration style with muted earthy colors (golden bronze, dark brown, bone white, hints of dark purple glow). Think medieval manuscript meets comic book inking. Thick dark outlines on everything for easy background removal.

Pure solid black background. NO background elements, NO patterns, NO atmospheric effects behind the logo. Clean edges everywhere.

Aspect ratio 3:2, 4K resolution.
```

## 2. Логотип с персонажами игры (промо / key art)

```
Create a fantasy video game logo for "HEXBOUND" with the composition of World of Warcraft expansion logos — decorative frame with title and characters — but drawn entirely in a detailed hand-drawn ink illustration and crosshatching art style with muted earthy colors on black background.

The word "HEXBOUND" in large hand-drawn fantasy lettering with golden-bronze color, crosshatch shading for depth, thick dark ink outlines, and rough organic hand-drawn edges. Faint rune engravings scratched into the letter surfaces.

Behind the text, an ornate hexagonal shield-frame drawn in ink crosshatch style — dark iron and bronze metalwork with rivets, filigree, thorny vines, all rendered in hand-drawn line art with crosshatching. A hexagonal dark purple gemstone with golden glow sits at the top.

Grotesque dark fantasy characters from the game interact with the frame — drawn in the same ink crosshatch style with muted colors:

LEFT SIDE: A red-skinned demon with small horns, black messy hair, yellow eyes, and a sly toothy grin leans around the frame edge. Below, a stocky green-skinned female orc with tusks, bone decorations in messy dark hair, and heavy leather armor grips the frame.

RIGHT SIDE: A skull-faced undead jester with wild hair, glowing yellow eyes, and a tattered colorful bell-hat drapes over the top of the frame. Below, a haggard witch with wild gray-purple hair, sunken eyes, missing teeth, and torn green robes reaches up clawing at the border.

BOTTOM CENTER: Bony skeleton hands with long cracked nails reach up from below, gripping the bottom edge of the frame.

Below the frame on a drawn banner: "FORGE YOUR FATE" in smaller matching ink lettering.

Everything in consistent hand-drawn ink and crosshatching illustration style. Character art matches the game's grotesque dark fantasy aesthetic — detailed crosshatching, muted desaturated earthy colors (reds, greens, purples, browns, bone whites), thick dark ink outlines on everything. NOT polished or clean — deliberately rough, hand-drawn, and full of dark personality.

Pure solid black background. No background elements. Thick outlines on all elements for clean background removal.

Aspect ratio 16:9, 4K resolution.
```

## 3. Иконка App Store

```
Create a square app icon for a dark fantasy RPG game, using the composition style of Hearthstone's app icon but drawn in hand-drawn ink illustration and crosshatching art style.

Center: a hexagonal frame made of hand-drawn ornate dark metal with ink crosshatch shading, bronze-gold filigree details, rivets, and thorny vines — all drawn in detailed ink line art style, not smooth digital. The frame has thick dark outlines.

Inside the hexagon, a grotesque cartoon face peers out — drawn in detailed ink and crosshatch style: enormous round bulging yellow eyes with tiny pinpoint pupils looking in slightly different directions, a very wide crooked grin with sharp uneven teeth and gaps, greenish-brown warty bumpy skin rendered with fine crosshatching. One bony clawed hand with cracked nails grips the top edge of the hexagonal frame, as if the creature is trying to escape.

Tiny details: small skull ornament at the bottom of the hexagon, faint golden glow lines around the frame edges drawn as radiating ink lines.

Background: very dark near-black. The icon must read clearly at very small sizes. Bold shapes, high contrast, thick ink outlines.

Style: hand-drawn ink illustration with crosshatching, muted earthy colors (golden-bronze frame, green-brown creature, bone white teeth and claws), dark comic book aesthetic. Square 1:1, 4K resolution.
```

## Советы

- **Для логин-экрана**: промпт #1 или #4
- **Для App Store**: промпт #3
- **Для промо/соцсети**: промпт #2 с персонажами
- **Для мрачного вайба**: промпт #5 каменный
- **Ключевое отличие от обычных Blizzard-промптов**: везде указано "hand-drawn ink illustration and crosshatching" вместо "polished 3D digital painting" — это держит всё в стиле арта твоих персонажей
- **Если AI всё равно делает слишком гладко**: добавь в конец "rough hand-drawn quality, visible ink strokes, NOT smooth, NOT polished, NOT 3D rendered"
- **Удаление фона**: чёрный фон + толстые outlines = легко убрать в любом редакторе
