# Hexbound — Промпты для генерации UI-иконок (экран выбора персонажа)

> **Status**: `canonical` — Active prompt collection. Indexed at `docs/08_prompts/ASSET_PROMPTS_INDEX.md`

> **Общий стиль для ВСЕХ UI-иконок:**
> Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, pen-and-ink linework with watercolor wash coloring, crosshatching shading technique, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines with varying line weight. Pure solid black background (#000000). Square 512×512px. NO text, NO characters — icon object only. Clean edges for easy cutout.

## 1. `ui-arrow-left` — Стрелка влево (навигация)

A single left-pointing navigation arrow icon for a dark fantasy RPG character selection screen. Pure solid black background (#000000). NO text, NO characters.

THE ARROW: A bold left-pointing chevron/arrow carved from dark forged iron. The arrowhead is angular and medieval — like a Gothic pointed arch turned sideways. Thick iron construction with visible hammer marks, small rivets along the spine, and fine crosshatch shading giving the metal weight and depth. The edges are slightly uneven — hand-forged, not machine-perfect. Faint greenish-bronze patina sits in the recesses and crevices of the metal.

MAGICAL ACCENTS: Faint golden-amber glow (#D4A537) emanates from thin runic engravings etched along the inner edge of the arrow. Tiny arcane symbols — barely visible — pulse with warm light along the arrow's surface. A subtle golden rim light traces the forward-pointing edge, suggesting magical responsiveness.

SURFACE DETAILS: Micro-scratches and battle wear on the iron surface. A small chain link fragment hangs from the tail end of the arrow. The iron has a cold blue-gray base tone with warm golden highlights where the runes glow.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark iron gray, greenish-bronze patina, golden glow (#D4A537) as the luminous accent, cold steel blue-gray base. Minimal palette — dark metal with gold magic contrast.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. Pure solid black background. Square 1:1, 512x512. NO text, NO characters — arrow icon only.

## 3. `ui-gender-male` — Значок мужского пола (♂)

> **ВАЖНО: Композиция зеркальна с `ui-gender-female`.** Оба символа строго вертикальны, центрированы по оси, одинакового размера и веса. Круг занимает нижнюю часть иконки, выступающий элемент (стрелка / крест) направлен строго вверх / вниз соответственно.

A single male gender symbol icon for a dark fantasy RPG character selection screen. The symbol is oriented STRICTLY VERTICALLY — the circle is centered in the lower half of the frame, and a single arrow points STRAIGHT UP from the top of the circle along the vertical center axis. Pure solid black background (#000000). NO text, NO characters.

COMPOSITION: The circle sits in the lower-center of the 512×512 frame. The arrow extends straight up from the 12 o'clock position of the ring, pointing directly upward. The entire symbol is perfectly centered on the vertical axis — mirror-symmetric left to right. This layout is designed to be a vertical mirror pair with the female symbol (which has the cross extending straight down from the bottom of the circle).

THE SYMBOL: The circle is a thick iron ring forged from dark metal — visible hammer marks, uneven hand-forged edges, and heavy rivets at the left and right cardinal points (3 and 9 o'clock). The arrow shaft is a thick iron bar extending straight up from the top of the ring, ending in a sharp medieval spearpoint arrowhead — angular, aggressive, like a Gothic lance tip. The shaft has crosshatch-shaded metallic texture. The overall proportions: the ring takes up roughly 60% of the icon height, the arrow takes the remaining 40% above.

MAGICAL ACCENTS: The entire symbol glows with a cold steel-blue magical energy (#4A9EDB). Faint blue-white arcane light pulses from within the iron, illuminating the edges with a cool cyan rim light. Tiny ice-blue runic marks are etched into the surface of the ring — ancient gender runes from a forgotten tongue. The arrowhead tip has a brighter concentration of blue-white light, like a focused magical charge. Small ice-blue magical sparks drift upward from the arrowhead.

SURFACE DETAILS: The iron surface has battle scars — sword nicks on the ring, scratches on the shaft. Greenish-bronze patina in the deepest grooves. A faint cold mist or vapor rises from the symbol. Small iron chain fragments dangle from the left and right sides of the ring — symmetrically placed.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark iron gray base, steel-blue magical glow (#4A9EDB), cyan-white highlights at the brightest points, greenish-bronze patina in recesses. Cold and strong palette.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. Pure solid black background. Square 1:1, 512x512. NO text, NO characters — symbol icon only.

## 5. `ui-dice` — Кубик костей (рандомизация)

A single six-sided die (D6) icon for a dark fantasy RPG character selection screen, representing randomization. Pure solid black background (#000000). NO text, NO characters.

THE DIE: A single six-sided die viewed at a three-quarter angle — showing three visible faces. The die is forged from dark iron with rounded but heavy edges. Each visible face has circular pip indentations (dots showing the number) — the pips are not painted but are drilled holes filled with glowing magical energy. The die is slightly larger than perfect proportions — chunky and weighty, like a cursed gambling artifact. The iron surface has crosshatch shading showing the metallic weight. Edges are slightly uneven — hand-forged, with tiny hammer marks and imperfections. Corner vertices have small iron thorns or spikes protruding — a sinister detail.

PIP DETAILS: The pips (dot indentations) on the top face show 6 (six glowing dots), the right face shows 3, and the left face shows 1 — standard D6 orientation. Each pip glows with golden-amber magical energy (#D4A537 core, #FFD700 brightest center). The glow spills slightly onto the iron surface around each pip, creating warm light pools. Faint arcane micro-symbols are scratched around each pip — tiny hexagonal patterns barely visible.

MAGICAL ACCENTS: The edges of the die have faint golden runic engravings — fate runes associated with chance and destiny. A subtle golden aura radiates from the die as a whole — the glow of an enchanted object. Tiny golden sparks or motes float in the air around the die, suggesting it was just thrown or is hovering with magical energy. One corner of the die drips with a single droplet of molten gold — as if the magical energy is so intense it melts the metal.

SURFACE DETAILS: Deep scratches and gouges on the iron faces — this die has been used in countless games of fate. A greenish-bronze patina in the recesses. Faint burn marks where the magical pips have scorched the surrounding iron. A broken chain link attached to one corner — as if the die was once chained to prevent theft.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark iron gray base, golden-amber magical glow (#D4A537, #FFD700) from the pips and runes, greenish-bronze patina, faint orange-warm tones in the deepest engravings. The signature gold-on-dark-iron Hexbound palette.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. Pure solid black background. Square 1:1, 512x512. NO text, NO characters — die icon only.

### Рекомендации по генерации

1. **Фон**: чистый чёрный (#000000) — для лёгкого вырезания
2. **Формат**: PNG, 512×512px
3. **Стиль**: Единообразный с остальными ассетами Hexbound — ink & watercolor, crosshatching, bold outlines
4. **В Xcode**: каждый ассет в `Assets.xcassets` как imageset (например, `ui-arrow-left.imageset`)
5. **Цветовое кодирование**: стрелки и кубик — золотой (#D4A537), мужской — голубой (#4A9EDB), женский — фиолетовый (#C850C0)
