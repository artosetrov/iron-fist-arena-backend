## 1. `hud-gift` — Подарок / Награды (Daily Rewards)

A single gift box icon for a dark fantasy RPG hub city HUD overlay. Pure solid black background (#000000). NO text, NO characters.

THE GIFT BOX: A small ornate treasure chest or gift box, viewed from a slight front-angled perspective. The box is forged from dark iron with a lid slightly raised — hinting at something glowing inside. The lid has a thick iron bow/ribbon on top, medieval in style — like two iron strips bent into loops and riveted together at the center, resembling a crude metallic gift bow. The box body is rectangular with reinforced iron corners, small decorative studs along the edges, and a heavy front clasp.

MAGICAL ACCENTS: A warm golden glow (#D4A537, #FFD700) emanates from the crack between the lid and the base — as if a powerful magical artifact is inside. The iron bow on top glows with the same golden energy along its edges. Tiny golden runes are engraved into the front face of the box — reward sigils. Small golden sparks and motes drift upward from the lid gap. The entire icon has a warm golden aura suggesting treasure and generosity.

SURFACE DETAILS: The iron surface has crosshatch shading showing weight and age. Greenish-bronze patina in the deepest recesses. Scratch marks and dents — this box has traveled far. A small broken chain link hangs from one side — the box was once locked away. The clasp on the front is slightly worn.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark iron gray base, golden magical glow (#D4A537, #FFD700) as the dominant luminous accent, greenish-bronze patina, warm amber tones in the deepest glow. The signature Hexbound gold-on-dark-iron palette.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. Pure solid black background. Square 1:1, 512x512. NO text, NO characters — gift box icon only.

## 2. `hud-quests` — Свиток квестов (Quest Log)

A single rolled parchment scroll icon for a dark fantasy RPG hub city HUD overlay. Pure solid black background (#000000). NO text, NO characters.

THE SCROLL: A partially unrolled parchment scroll, viewed from a slight front-angled perspective. The scroll is held together by dark iron end caps — cylindrical ferrules with small spikes at the tips, forged iron with visible hammer marks. The parchment itself is aged and yellowed — thick vellum with torn edges and burn marks along the borders. The scroll is partially unfurled, showing a glimpse of the inner surface where faint runic text and quest markings are visible.

MAGICAL ACCENTS: The visible text on the inner parchment glows with warm golden energy (#D4A537) — active quests pulsing with magical urgency. A golden seal or wax stamp sits at the top of the visible parchment area — an official quest authority mark. Faint golden light spills from between the rolled layers of the scroll. Small golden dust particles float around the scroll, suggesting ancient magical knowledge.

SURFACE DETAILS: The parchment has ink stains, small holes from age, and visible fiber texture in the vellum. The iron end caps have crosshatch shading and greenish-bronze patina. A thin iron chain or leather cord is loosely wrapped around the scroll to keep it closed — partially undone. A small iron hook or clasp at the center where the cord attaches.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark iron gray end caps, aged yellow-brown parchment, golden magical glow (#D4A537, #FFD700) from the quest text, greenish-bronze patina on the iron parts, warm amber tones. The quest scroll feels ancient and important.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. Pure solid black background. Square 1:1, 512x512. NO text, NO characters — scroll icon only.

## 3. `hud-sound-on` — Звук включён

A single classic speaker/volume icon stylized as a dark fantasy medieval artifact, for a dark fantasy RPG hub city HUD overlay, representing sound/music ENABLED. The icon is CENTERED on the canvas — perfectly centered both horizontally and vertically within the 512×512 frame. Pure solid black background (#000000). NO text, NO characters.

COMPOSITION: The speaker icon sits exactly in the center of the canvas. Equal black space on all sides. The speaker with its sound waves together form a compact, balanced composition that does not drift to any edge. The entire object (speaker + waves) occupies roughly 60–70% of the frame, centered.

THE SPEAKER: The classic speaker silhouette shape — a trapezoidal speaker cone on the right connected to a smaller rectangular base on the left (the universal audio icon shape). The entire shape is built from forged dark iron — not a smooth modern icon but a hand-forged medieval iron object. The rectangular base is a thick iron block with visible rivets at the corners and crosshatch shading. The trapezoidal cone flares outward to the right, made of hammered iron plates joined with small rivets along the seams. The edges are slightly uneven — hand-forged, not machine-perfect. The opening of the cone has a reinforced iron rim.

SOUND WAVES: Three curved arc lines emanate from the right side of the speaker cone, representing active sound at full volume. These arcs are made of golden magical energy (#D4A537, #FFD700) — glowing, luminous, like visible sound waves made of light. The arcs expand outward, each one larger and fainter than the last. Tiny golden runic glyphs float within the wave arcs — arcane sound symbols.

SURFACE DETAILS: The iron surface has crosshatch shading showing metallic weight. Greenish-bronze patina in every recess, rivet groove, and seam joint. Fine engraved runic patterns along the speaker cone — sound-amplification enchantments. Small scratches and dents from age and use. A small broken chain link dangles from the bottom of the base.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark iron gray body, golden magical glow (#D4A537, #FFD700) for the sound waves, greenish-bronze patina. Warm and active — the speaker is alive with sound.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. Pure solid black background. Square 1:1, 512x512. Icon CENTERED on canvas. NO text, NO characters — speaker icon only.

## 4. `hud-sound-off` — Звук выключен (Mute)

A single classic speaker/volume icon with a diagonal slash through it, stylized as a dark fantasy medieval artifact, for a dark fantasy RPG hub city HUD overlay, representing sound/music DISABLED (muted). The icon is CENTERED on the canvas — perfectly centered both horizontally and vertically within the 512×512 frame. Pure solid black background (#000000). NO text, NO characters.

COMPOSITION: The muted speaker icon sits exactly in the center of the canvas. Equal black space on all sides. The speaker with its diagonal slash together form a compact, balanced composition that does not drift to any edge. The entire object (speaker + slash) occupies roughly 60–70% of the frame, centered. Same centering and scale as `hud-sound-on`.

THE SPEAKER: Identical speaker shape to `hud-sound-on` — the same classic speaker silhouette (trapezoidal cone + rectangular base) forged from dark iron. Same construction, same rivets, same proportions, same centered position. Same hand-forged medieval iron aesthetic.

SILENCE INDICATOR: Instead of golden sound wave arcs, a bold diagonal iron bar crosses over the entire speaker — forged dark iron, stretched diagonally from upper-right to lower-left. This slash is a thick iron mute bar with its own rivets and crosshatch shading — a physical magical seal. A single clear diagonal slash indicates muted state. NO golden sound waves, NO golden arcs, NO golden particles. The cone opening is dark and empty.

MUTED STATE: The entire speaker has a colder, more desaturated tone compared to the sound-on version. The iron shifts toward a dull blue-gray — the warmth is completely drained. The engraved runes along the cone are dimmed — barely visible, almost extinguished. A faint cold blue-gray tint (#4A5568) replaces any warm glow. Thin frost-like crystalline marks appear around the cone opening — as if the sound has been magically frozen and sealed.

SURFACE DETAILS: Same iron construction as `hud-sound-on`. The diagonal mute bar has its own rivets where it crosses the speaker body. Heavier greenish-bronze patina overall — as if disuse has accelerated the aging. The chain link hangs limply.

COLOR PALETTE: Desaturated dark iron gray (cooler than the on version), cold blue-gray accents (#4A5568), heavy greenish-bronze patina. NO golden glow — the icon reads as cold, inactive, and silent.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. Pure solid black background. Square 1:1, 512x512. Icon CENTERED on canvas. NO text, NO characters — muted speaker icon only.
