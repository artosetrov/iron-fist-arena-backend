# Hexbound — Промпты для Nano Banana Pro: Loading Screens

> **Status**: `canonical` — Active prompt collection. Indexed at `docs/08_prompts/ASSET_PROMPTS_INDEX.md`

## Общие правила для ВСЕХ loading screens

**Формат:** 9:19.5 (вертикальный, iPhone full-screen) — можно генерировать 9:16 и обрезать
**Фон:** Тёмный (#0D0D12 → #1A1A2E), чтобы лого и спиннер поверх были читаемы
**Стиль:** Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines.
**Важно:** Центр композиции должен быть слегка затемнён / пуст — там будет лого Hexbound и индикатор загрузки. Основной арт — по краям и в верхней/нижней трети.
**БЕЗ текста, БЕЗ надписей, БЕЗ UI элементов** — только иллюстрация.

## 1. `loading-pvp` — PvP Arena Loading Screen

A dark vertical illustration for a mobile game loading screen. The scene depicts a gladiatorial arena viewed from below, looking up — as if the player is walking out of the dark tunnel into the fighting pit.

COMPOSITION: The image is a vertical tunnel opening — dark stone walls on left and right narrowing toward a bright opening at the top third. The center-bottom half is deliberately dark and empty (UI overlay zone). The action and detail are concentrated at the top and along the edges.

TOP AREA (the arena opening): Harsh golden torchlight (#D4A537) spills down from above. The silhouette of a massive hexagonal arena cage is visible against a dark stormy sky. Iron bars, chains, and spiked gates frame the opening. Tattered battle banners hang from the walls — torn fabric with faded hexagonal emblems. Faint crowd silhouettes line the upper edge, grotesque misshapen figures cheering from behind iron railings.

LEFT WALL: Dark rough-hewn stone blocks with deep cracks. Iron torch brackets with flickering flames casting warm orange light. Scratched tally marks gouged into the stone by past fighters. A battered shield hung on the wall as a trophy — dented, blood-stained.

RIGHT WALL: Similar stone, but with chains and manacles bolted to the wall. A rack of weapons visible in the shadows — axes, maces, broken swords. Dried blood splatters on the stone, rendered in dark rust-brown with crosshatch detail. A wanted poster (illegible, just shapes) nailed to the wall.

BOTTOM EDGE: The stone floor of the tunnel — cracked flagstones with sand and debris. Boot prints and drag marks in the dust. A few scattered gold coins glinting in the torchlight. The player's shadow stretches forward toward the arena.

COLOR PALETTE: Very dark — deep charcoal (#1A1A2E), warm stone brown, golden-orange torchlight (#D4A537), rust-red blood accents, iron gray. The golden light at the top creates a dramatic gradient from dark bottom to bright top.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. NO text, NO UI elements, NO characters facing the viewer. Vertical composition 9:16.

## 2. `loading-dungeon` — Dungeon Loading Screen

A dark vertical illustration for a mobile game loading screen. The scene depicts the entrance to an ancient underground dungeon — a descending stone staircase disappearing into darkness.

COMPOSITION: Vertical descent — the stairs go from the top of the image downward into blackness at the bottom. The center is kept relatively dark (UI overlay zone). Detail is concentrated along the edges and the upper portion.

TOP AREA: A crumbling stone archway with a hexagonal keystone at its apex — the dungeon entrance. Ancient carved runes glow faintly along the arch in sickly green and dull purple. Cobwebs stretch between the stones. Twisted dead roots push through cracks in the ceiling. A single iron lantern hangs from a chain, casting weak warm light that barely reaches a few steps down.

LEFT EDGE: Damp stone walls covered in moss and lichen, drawn with dense crosshatching. Mushrooms with faintly bioluminescent caps grow in clusters from cracks — pale blue-green glow. A human skull embedded in the wall, half-covered by moss — a past adventurer who didn't return. Dripping water — small stalactites with water droplets caught mid-fall.

RIGHT EDGE: More stone wall, but with ancient carved reliefs — grotesque faces with open mouths (drain gargoyles), partially eroded. A massive spider web stretches across a side passage, with a dark shape lurking at its center. Iron brackets where torches once were — now empty and rusted. Scratch marks from claws gouged deep into the stone.

CENTER-BOTTOM: The staircase descends into pure darkness. The last few visible steps are cracked, some broken away entirely. A faint ominous glow — deep red-orange — emanates from far below, barely visible. Scattered bones on the steps. A dropped adventurer's pack, half-open, with a broken potion bottle leaking green liquid down the stairs.

COLOR PALETTE: Very dark — near-black (#0D0D12) dominant. Sickly green bioluminescence, dull purple rune glow, weak warm lantern light at top, deep red-orange hint from below. Damp, cold, oppressive.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. NO text, NO UI elements. Vertical composition 9:16.

## 3. `loading-hub` — Hub City / General Loading Screen

A dark vertical illustration for a mobile game loading screen. The scene depicts a narrow medieval alley in a dark fantasy city at night — the path from the city gate to the central plaza.

COMPOSITION: A vertical alley corridor — crooked buildings lean inward from both sides, creating a narrow canyon of architecture. The center has a path of dim light leading forward. The middle zone is kept darker (UI overlay zone). Detail is dense along the edges.

TOP AREA: A sliver of dark night sky visible between the leaning rooftops. A pale crescent moon partially hidden by ragged clouds. Chimney smoke curls upward. Gargoyles and grotesque carved faces perch on the roofline — ink-drawn stone creatures with exaggerated features, watching the alley below. A tattered guild banner hangs between buildings on a rope — hexagonal emblem barely visible.

LEFT SIDE: A crooked timber-and-stone building facade. A tavern sign hangs from an iron bracket — a wooden plaque with a carved tankard (no text). Warm amber light spills from a small window, casting a rectangle of light onto the cobblestones. A barrel and crates stacked outside. Rats scurrying near the barrel base. A wanted poster board with torn papers.

RIGHT SIDE: A darker building — a shop with iron-barred windows. Mysterious glowing bottles visible behind the bars — an alchemist's shop. A lantern on an iron pole casts a pool of greenish light. A stray cat's eyes glow from a shadow between buildings. Vines and moss creep up the stonework. A drain grate in the wall drips dark liquid.

BOTTOM AREA: Wet cobblestones reflecting the ambient light — puddles from recent rain catch the warm tavern glow and the greenish alchemist light. Scattered debris — a broken bottle, a lost coin, crumpled paper. Cart wheel tracks in the mud between the stones. The cobblestones are uneven, cracked, patched with different stone — old city, many repairs.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark — near-black (#0D0D12) shadows, warm amber tavern light, greenish alchemist glow, pale moonlight blue at the top, dark stone gray, wet cobblestone reflections. Atmospheric and moody.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. NO text, NO UI elements, NO characters. Vertical composition 9:16.

## 4. `loading-shop` — Shop / Inventory Loading Screen

A dark vertical illustration for a mobile game loading screen. The scene depicts the interior of a dark fantasy merchant's shop — shelves, weapons, potions, and treasures filling the frame.

COMPOSITION: Vertical — towering shelves and display racks fill the image from bottom to top. The center is slightly less cluttered (UI overlay zone). Items and details are packed along the edges and corners.

TOP AREA: The ceiling of the shop — heavy wooden beams with iron hooks. Weapons hang from the ceiling on chains — a massive war axe, a pair of crossed swords, a morning star. Bundles of dried herbs tied with string hang in rows. A stuffed creature (taxidermy monster head — grotesque horned beast with glass eyes) mounted on a plaque. Cobwebs in the upper corners.

LEFT SHELF WALL: Floor-to-ceiling wooden shelves crammed with items — glass potion bottles of every color (red health, blue mana, green poison, golden buff), leather-bound books, jars with preserved specimens (an eye, a finger, teeth), stacked scrolls, a crystal ball on a brass stand, boxes with items spilling out. Each shelf sags slightly under the weight. Small price tags (illegible scribbles) hang from strings.

RIGHT DISPLAY AREA: A weapon rack with swords, daggers, and a staff. An armor stand with a battered breastplate and chainmail. A glass display case with rings, amulets, and gems on velvet — faint magical glow from some items. A locked iron chest on the floor, heavy padlock, mysterious. Boots and gloves piled on a lower shelf.

BOTTOM AREA: The shop counter — thick dark wood with iron reinforcement. A brass scale for weighing gold. A thick ledger book open to a page of scribbled transactions. A coin purse spilling gold hexagonal coins onto the counter. A small oil lamp on the counter provides warm light. Below the counter — more storage, partially visible.

COLOR PALETTE: Warm and rich — amber lamplight, golden coin glints, colorful potion bottles providing small splashes of color against dark wood and iron. Dark background (#0D0D12), warm wood browns, brass-gold (#D4A537) accents. Cozy but slightly sinister.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. NO text, NO UI elements, NO characters. Vertical composition 9:16.

## 5. `loading-battle-pass` — Battle Pass / Seasonal Loading Screen

A dark vertical illustration for a mobile game loading screen. The scene depicts a mythical forge or ritual chamber — representing the seasonal Battle Pass progression and reward crafting.

COMPOSITION: Vertical — a central forge/anvil area with mystical energy rising upward. The center-middle zone is kept darker and emptier (UI overlay zone). Dramatic elements at top and bottom.

TOP AREA: A vaulted stone ceiling with a hexagonal oculus (opening) at the center — through it, a beam of pale moonlight or arcane energy descends. The beam refracts into golden particles as it hits the forge below. Ancient runes carved into the ceiling stones glow with pulsing golden light (#FFD700). Chains hang from the vault — heavy iron, some with hooks, some with mysterious suspended artifacts (a glowing orb, a sealed cage with something moving inside).

LEFT SIDE: A massive bellows mechanism — leather and iron, operated by thick chains. The wall behind it is covered in weapon molds, templates, and blueprints pinned to the stone. A shelf of raw materials — ingots of different metals (dull iron, gleaming gold, dark obsidian, purple enchanted crystal). A crucible with molten metal glowing orange-red.

RIGHT SIDE: A wall of completed rewards displayed on hooks and stands — legendary weapons with golden glow, ornate armor pieces, glowing amulets, a crown on a velvet cushion. Each item has a faint magical aura. Below them, locked display cases with rare items barely visible through frosted glass — the premium track rewards. A progression chart carved into the wall (abstract marks, no readable text).

BOTTOM AREA: The central forge — a massive iron anvil sitting on a hexagonal stone platform. Glowing embers surround the base. A heavy war hammer rests against the anvil. Scattered around: metal shavings, cooling tongs, quenching water bucket with steam rising. The stone floor has hexagonal tile patterns. Small magical runes etched into the anvil glow gold.

COLOR PALETTE: Dark base (#0D0D12), rich golden glow (#FFD700, #D4A537) as dominant accent — this is the most golden of all loading screens. Orange-red forge embers, purple crystal accents for rare items. The gold represents premium rewards and progression.

Hand-drawn ink and watercolor illustration, crosshatching shading, grotesque dark fantasy medieval manuscript art style. Thick bold outlines. NO text, NO UI elements, NO characters. Vertical composition 9:16.
