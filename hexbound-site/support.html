FAQ

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account", or email us and we'll process your request within 30 days.

I lost my progress. Can you help?
If you were signed in with Google or Apple, your progress is saved on our servers. Simply sign in again with the same account.

The app is crashing. What should I do?
Try updating to the latest version from the App Store. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.

Account Deletion

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing osetrov.artem@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.
